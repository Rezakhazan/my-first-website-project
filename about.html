About Us

Born in 1983 in Tehran, I left Iran in 2011. My passion to astronomy and astophotography started when I was 13, with the first look to the moon through a telescope. Later I became fascinated by the universality of the night sky; the unifying roof above the symbols of our world. I studied mining engineering and later on continued my education in petroleum field when I came to the UK. However, my strong passion to astronomy and magnificence of the night sky led me to attend many scientific stargazing events and astronomy bootcamps.I was lucky enough to get to know some of the best and knowledgable speakers and explorers.
